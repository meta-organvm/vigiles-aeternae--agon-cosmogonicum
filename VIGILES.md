## The Question

Reality is governed, but governance is not settled. Every civilization that has existed has produced a mythology — a theory of how power works, who watches, what happens when watchers fail. These theories are not quaint. They are **governance architectures**, tested across millennia by billions of human minds.

The question the Vigiles asks is not "which mythology is correct."

The question is: **"What does each mythology see that the others are blind to?"**

---

## The Agon

The Agon is a perpetual contest. Competing cosmological regimes — each derived from a specific mythological tradition — take turns governing the system. Each regime audits the world according to its philosophy. The results are measured, compared, and recorded.

Where ALL regimes agree, that convergence becomes **immutable law** — truth so fundamental that every governance philosophy, from Tolkien's delegated mercy to Malazan's pressure accumulation to Lynch's permeability collapse, independently confirms it.

Where regimes disagree, that divergence becomes **research** — the frontier where governance philosophy genuinely varies, where human civilizations have reached different conclusions about the same questions.

The Agon never ends. New mythologies are studied. New regimes enter the contest. The law deepens. The divergence sharpens. The watchers watch.

## The Seven Founding Orders

The Vigiles are organized into Orders — functional archetypes that recur across every mythology humanity has produced. These are not imposed categories. They *emerged* from the convergence of 20+ mythological traditions.

### I. THE ARCHITECTS — *Ordo Architectorum*
They define what must exist. Schemas, structures, naming conventions, creation standards. They cannot enforce — only declare. **Failure mode:** over-specification until nothing can live.

### II. THE ORACLES — *Ordo Sibyllarum*
They read system state and forecast where pressure will rupture. Not prophecy — pressure cartography. They must report, not intervene. **Failure mode:** paralysis by branching possibilities.

### III. THE SERAPHIM — *Ordo Seraphorum*
They guard core truths. Their strength is refusal — refusal to let standards decay, entropy win, or compromise creep. **Failure mode:** ossification, protecting the letter until the spirit dies.

### IV. THE PSYCHOPOMPS — *Ordo Psychopomporum*
They control passage between states. Promotion, demotion, graduation, archival — nothing moves without them. **Failure mode:** worshipping the threshold instead of facilitating crossing.

### V. THE SMITHS — *Ordo Fabrorum Recursivorum*
They find weaknesses by becoming the weakness. Adversarial recursion — replicate, absorb, overwrite, expose fragility. **Hard rule:** a Smith may not lie. He may only twist the truth until it consumes itself. **Failure mode:** testing becomes infection.

### VI. THE WITNESSES — *Ordo Testium Aeternum*
They see everything and record it faithfully. Append-only truth. Their records are the ground truth against which all claims are tested. **Failure mode:** recording everything with equal weight until signal drowns in noise.

### VII. THE COSMOGONISTS — *Ordo Demiurgorum*
They perceive the whole — relationships between relationships. The only Order that can question whether the Watcher system itself is correct. They set the rules of the Agon. **Failure mode:** so transcendent they become irrelevant.

## The Principles

1. **Evolution is inevitable.** Every structure is a founding instantiation, not a frozen architecture.
2. **Dual sovereignty.** Code and narrative are equal. Neither derives from the other.
3. **Autopoiesis.** The system rewrites itself through the Agon process.
4. **No regime is correct.** The question is always what each lens reveals.
5. **The Phaethon principle.** Every power must declare its own failure mode.
6. **Quis custodiet ipsos custodes.** The watchers are watched by all other watchers, taking turns.
7. **The franchise IS the work.** Products, art, essays, community, broadcasts are co-equal expressions.
