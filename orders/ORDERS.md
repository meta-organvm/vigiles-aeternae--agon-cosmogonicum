# The Eight Orders of the Vigiles Aeternae

Eight functional archetypes — seven founding, one research-emergent — each simultaneously a governance function, an RPG faction, and a mythological archetype. Orders are numbered independently of ORGANVM organ numbering. Orders and Regimes are orthogonal: every Order audits under every Regime; every Regime is audited by every Order.

## Hierarchy

```
VII. COSMOGONISTS (meta-systemic — watches the watchers)
  ├── I.    ARCHITECTS    (design law)
  ├── II.   ORACLES       (predictive sensing)
  ├── III.  SERAPHIM      (active protection)
  ├── IV.   PSYCHOPOMPS   (state transitions)
  ├── V.    SMITHS        (adversarial testing)
  ├── VI.   WITNESSES     (immutable recording)
  └── VIII. NATARAJA      (sacred dissolution) ← RESEARCH-EMERGENT
```

The Cosmogonists sit above. The other seven are peers — no hierarchy among them, each supreme in its domain, each blind in the others'. The tension between Orders IS the Agon at the micro level.

The Nataraja (Order VIII) was not part of the founding seven. It emerged from the research corpus when four independent traditions (Greek Furies, Marvel Galactus, Hindu Shiva, Egyptian Ammit) converged on a governance function the founders didn't cover: **sacred dissolution — the ending that enables new beginning**. This is the first demonstration of the Agon reshaping its own governance structure through research.

## Order Files

Each `.yaml` file in this directory defines one Order with: identity, domain, powers, constraints, failure modes, RPG stats, system functions, and mythological sources.

## Evolution

The eight Orders are the current instantiation. Research will continue to reshape them. The Nataraja's emergence proves the process works — mythology study reveals governance functions that design-from-first-principles misses. Further candidates under investigation: Trickster Order (Norse Loki, Yoruba Eshu), Blessing/Enablement Order (Abrahamic Virtues). Evolution is inevitable — that is the first principle.
