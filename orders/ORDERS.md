# The Seven Founding Orders of the Vigiles Aeternae

Seven functional archetypes that recur across every mythology humanity has produced. Each is simultaneously a governance function, an RPG faction, and a mythological archetype. Orders are numbered independently of ORGANVM organ numbering. Orders and Regimes are orthogonal: every Order audits under every Regime; every Regime is audited by every Order.

## Hierarchy

```
VII. COSMOGONISTS (meta-systemic — watches the watchers)
  ├── I.  ARCHITECTS    (design law)
  ├── II. ORACLES       (predictive sensing)
  ├── III. SERAPHIM     (active protection)
  ├── IV. PSYCHOPOMPS   (state transitions)
  ├── V.  SMITHS        (adversarial testing)
  └── VI. WITNESSES     (immutable recording)
```

The Cosmogonists sit above. The other six are peers — no hierarchy among them, each supreme in its domain, each blind in the others'. The tension between Orders IS the Agon at the micro level.

## Order Files

Each `.yaml` file in this directory defines one Order with: identity, domain, powers, constraints, failure modes, RPG stats, system functions, and mythological sources.

## Evolution

These seven are the founding instantiation. Research will reshape them. New Orders may emerge (the Furies? Galactus-type necessary destruction? The Trickster?). Existing Orders may split or merge. Evolution is inevitable — that is the first principle.
